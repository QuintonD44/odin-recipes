Pasta with Meat Sauce

This is an easy and affordable weeknight recipe that the whole family is sure to love!

Serves 3-4 people

Ingredients

  • 1lb 80/20 Ground Beef
  • 1 medium yellow onion - medium chop
  • 4 large garlic cloves - minced
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1tsp garlic powder
  • 1tsp onion powder
  • 1tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1tsp oregano
  • 1tsp thyme
  • 2 bay leaves
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 1tbs cooking oil
  • 1/4lb pasta of your choise
  • Parmesean cheese (optional)
  • Chicken stock (optional)

Directions

Meat Sauce

  1. Preheat a large pot or dutch oven over medium/high heat
  2. Add cooking oil
  3. Once the oil is hot, add the ground beef making sure as much surface area touches the bottom of the pot as possible. Add salt. Do not stir.
  4. after ~6 minutes, add the onion, garlic, herbs and spices. Stir with a wooden spoon or spatula.
  5. Once the onion has become slightly tranclucent and the garlic, herbs and spice are fragrant, pour in the marinara. Make sure you scrape up all the brown bits off the bottom of the pan. This is where a lot of the flavor is!
  6. Simmer for as little or as long as you want. This is the beauty of this recipe! once the pot is simmering, dinner can be served at your convenience. The longer you let the sauce simmer, the more developed and concentrated the flavor wil be. For the sake of example, lets let this ride for an hour or so
  7. If the sauce starts to dry up, just add a little chicken stock. If that's not around, water will do just fine

Pasta

  1. Bring 4 quarts of salted water to boil
  2. add pasta
  3. cook until just under al dente
  4. set a cup of starchy pasta water off to the side

Finishing Touches (Dont skip this step)

  1. preheat an oiled 12 inch skillet over medium heat
  2. Add scoops of meat sauce until bottom of the pan is covered
  3. Add pasta to the pan
  4. Add pasta water
  5. Simmer, stirring frequently until the sauce coats the pasta and pasta is cooked through
  6. Plate and serve, adding fresh parmesean if desired
